ABOUT US

FAWASTUDIOS:

FawaStudios first made it's entry into the game industry to fulfill the creative itch of an avid gamer, with the goal of creating a ton of cool and unique experiences. Beginning in 2017 (under the FawaStudios name), a large amount of nearly lost-to-time projects were created, some bad, and some downright horrible. However, amidst that steaming pile of garbage came some very valuable lessons, and a lot of experience.

Flashing forward, the FawaStudios train is still chugging along in pursuit of its original goal, with no plans to stop. We (being me) sincerely hope you come aboard and join us as we continue to grow, create, and (hopefully) entertain.

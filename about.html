ABOUT US

FawaStudios is an independent game developer and publisher based in the United States with a passion for creating all sorts of games. It was founded and currently operated by Ethan Fulmer (me), and I began by creating simple test projects in 2017 using the FawaStudios name.

In 2019, the concept of my main and largest project yet, Clown Clear, was created. Although over the years there have been several changes in vision and planning, I have stood by it and have been working on it since.

From the beginning of FawaStudios to current day, many projects have been worked on including a simple clicker game called Click Or Treat, and I have many plans for the future of game development and FawaStudios.

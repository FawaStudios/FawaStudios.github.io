ABOUT US

FawaStudios is an independent game developer and publisher based in United States with a passion for creating all sorts of games. It is founded and operated by me, Ethan Fulmer, and I began by creating very small and simple test projects in 2017 using the FawaStudios name.

In 2019, the concept of my main and largest project yet, Clown Clear, was created. Although within the time between then and now there have been several changes in vision and planning, I have stood by it and have been working on it since.

From the beginning of FawaStudios to current day, many projects have been worked on including a simple clicker game called Click Or Treat as a Halloween special, and I have many plans for the future of this small but meaningful studio.

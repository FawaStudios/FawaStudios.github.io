WHAT'S CLICK OR TREAT?

Click Or Treat is a Halloween themed clicker game involving the purchasing of monsters in order to get you more candy which was made originally in 7 days that released on October 31, 2021, it was the first "official" game published by FawaStudios, although there have been a few test projects that came before it.

Upon it's release, it was posted on GameJolt, Newgrounds, Itch.io, and the Clown Clear website.
